About me!

My name is Alexandra Dominguez, I am twenty and I have always had an affinity for traveling.

I have had the privilege of traveling to various locations across the world, and hope to expand the list of countries and places I have been in the future.

What I enjoy the most about traveling is the opportunity to immerse myself in different cultures, communities, and food.

I hope that this website can convey my love of traveling and adequately portray the beautiful cultures and places I have seen.

I hope this can be conveyed through the pictures I took of both myslef and the scenery to capture the essence of each place I have traveled!

I'm passionate about sharing my travel experiences and inspiring others to explore the world around them.
